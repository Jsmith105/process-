Research on Cancer Association of Spartanburg and cancer research

The Cancer Association of Spartanburg is a non-profit organization in Spartanburg that ptovides services to patents suffering from cancer. This includs home care supplies, loans for hospital equipment and assistance with medications. The Cancer Association is involed in many fairs and events as well as presentations at clubs. Cancer Association throws free events like the smoking cessation class. The Cancer Association is active on facebook, twitter, youtube channel and website. Cancer Association fundrases events in spartanburg, the newest event being "OVER THE EDGE UPSTATE".

Over the edge upstate event Ad on facebook.

This event is sponcered by United Community Bank that is benefiting the Cancer Association. The goal is to raise over $1000 to rappel over the edge of a 10 story building for the Cancer Association. the event will be held Friday, October 18, 2019.
